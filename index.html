Join as a supporter — it takes 10 seconds!

Link your Plussa card to K‑Supermarket Sammonlahti and support LUTES automatically. Every time you shop, 1 % of your purchases grows our sponsorship fund — at no extra cost to you.

If the automatic link doesn't open, you can add the code manually using the guide below.

💡 Add the code manually in the K‑Ruoka app (fallback)

  1. Open the K‑Ruoka app and sign in.
  2. Go to Profile → Settings → Manage your Plussa membership and choose Join as a supporter.
  3. Enter the code SP10076 and confirm. Done!

Web fallback: After login, open Sponsorship targets and click Become a supporter.

🚀 Why it matters

Your everyday groceries help fund student entrepreneurship events, startup programs and study trips organized by LUTES — without costing you a cent.

🧮 Example impact

If 150 supporters spend €100/month, LUTES receives about €1,800/year from K‑Supermarket Sammonlahti.
